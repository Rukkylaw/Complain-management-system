Brief History Of Student Affairs Unit

The student affairs unit is one of the units under the vice chancellor’s office which ensures that student’s welfare in term of health facilities, hostel accommodation, electricity and water supply as well as smooth inter campus transportation systems are provided. This unit also ensures peaceful co-existence among the student with their diverse ethnic, cultural and religious background on the two campuses. Professors with varied experience, who are the vice chancellor’s nominees, are normally appointed Deans of Student Affairs. They advise the vice chancellor on issues relating to student’s welfare and conduct. The student affairs unit ensures that the image of the University is portrayed in good light locally, Nationally and internationally. Apart from the administrative staff, the sub-deans of Student Affairs who are senior academic staff nominated by the vice chancellor also assist the Dean of Student Affairs in running the affairs of the unit. Hall Masters and Hall Mistresses also advice The Dean of student affairs on matters relating to welfare of students in their hall of residence. The current Dean of Student Affairs is Professor L.T. AJIBADE while the sub-dean is Dr. ALEX AKANMU.

Complaint Handled by the Unit

The Student Affairs division of the University handles students complaint on matters pertaining to their welfare on and off the campus, security, transportation, NYSC mobilization and hostel accomodation. The unit also have a department called the SDC which is the Student Deciplinary Committee and is responsible for disciplining students found to have committed any offence such as theft or examination malpractice amongst other things.
